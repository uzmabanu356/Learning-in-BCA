Computer

A computer is a machine that helps us do many things quickly and easily. It can solve problems, store information, and show pictures or videos. A computer has a screen, a keyboard, and a mouse to help us use it. People use computers for studying, working, playing games, and talking to friends online. Computers are very useful and make our work and life easier.

Mobile

A mobile is a small device that we can carry in our pocket. It is also called a smartphone. We use it to make calls, send messages, and take pictures. Mobiles can connect to the internet, so we can watch videos, play games, and talk to friends online. They are very handy and help us do many things quickly, no matter where we are.
